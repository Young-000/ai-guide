**Summary**: Anthropic shipped a Spotify Wrapped-style usage dashboard for all Claude users, complete with break nudges and quiet-hours controls, framing AI overconsumption as a product feature to address.

### Key Points
- Launched July 9 in beta for Free, Pro, and Max subscribers — no waitlist required
- Shows chat volume, topic distribution, and peak-usage hours across 1, 3, 6, or 12-month windows
- Quiet Hours and usage-limit nudges let users set boundaries on their AI time
- Incognito chats and health-tool data are excluded from analytics by design
- Requires Memory to be enabled in Claude Settings to access

### Why It Matters
Claude Reflect is Anthropic's most direct acknowledgment that AI dependency is a real user concern — but it also functions as a retention tool, reinforcing subscription value by surfacing what users would lose. If the digital-wellbeing framing resonates, expect rivals to ship similar dashboards soon.
