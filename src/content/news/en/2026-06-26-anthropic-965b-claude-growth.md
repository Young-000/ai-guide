**Summary**: Anthropic has closed a financing round at a $965 billion valuation — edging past OpenAI — and quietly filed for an IPO, as its flagship products post some of the fastest revenue growth in enterprise software history.

### Key Facts
- **$965B valuation** — latest funding round values Anthropic above OpenAI's March private valuation of $852 billion
- **Confidential IPO filing** — S-1 submitted to the SEC; no listing timeline announced yet
- **Claude revenue run-rate** — crossed $30 billion ARR in Q1 2026, representing ~80× year-over-year growth
- **Claude Code** — the agentic coding tool reached $2.5 billion ARR just nine months after launch

### Why It Matters
Anthropic has turned its safety-first positioning into a genuine enterprise advantage, winning large contracts where risk management matters. Claude Code's rapid ARR trajectory suggests that AI coding assistants are no longer a feature — they're a standalone product category worth billions. With both Anthropic and OpenAI heading toward IPOs, the competitive pressure on Google, Microsoft, and Meta to match their pace is only going to intensify.
