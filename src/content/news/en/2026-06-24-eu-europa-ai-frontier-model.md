**One-liner**: Europe's Frontier AI Grand Challenge has a winner — the Domyn-led EUROPA consortium will build a 400B+ parameter open-source model fluent in all 24 official EU languages.

### Key Facts
- **EUROPA consortium** led by Italian startup Domyn; European Commission announcement on June 19, 2026
- Target: open-source general-purpose model exceeding **400 billion parameters**, comparable to GPT-4-class systems
- Compute award: up to **2.5% of EuroHPC's full HPC capacity** for one year, including a dedicated 6,000-chip NVIDIA Blackwell cluster
- Covers all 24 EU official languages — a multilingual breadth no US frontier lab has matched at this parameter scale

### Why It Matters
Europe has long relied on US and Chinese AI infrastructure, routing sensitive public-sector queries through foreign servers. EUROPA would give EU institutions an auditable, sovereign alternative — open-source by design. It's also a signal that Europe has the engineering talent and compute infrastructure to compete at frontier scale, not just regulate it.
