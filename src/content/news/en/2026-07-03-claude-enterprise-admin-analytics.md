**Summary**: Anthropic released a new suite of admin controls for Claude Enterprise on July 2, 2026, including a per-user cost dashboard, model-level access entitlements, and automatic spend-threshold alerts.

### Key Points
- Analytics dashboard now shows usage and cost by group and individual user, with artifacts created, files edited, and skills used displayed alongside their dollar costs
- Spend-threshold alerts notify admins at 75% and 90% of the org-level spend limit; users receive in-app notifications at 75% and 95% and can request a limit increase without leaving Claude
- Model-level entitlements let admins route specific models (e.g., Fable 5 for engineering teams, Sonnet 5 for everyone else), enabling tiered cost and access governance by department
- Admin API enables automating cost-control workflows at scale—flagging members nearing limits and automating increase-request reviews across large organizations

### Why It Matters
With Fable 5 back on global access and per-seat AI spending rising, budget governance has become a blocking issue for enterprise IT. These controls directly address the gap between AI pilot projects and org-wide deployments: departments need to see where money is going, set guardrails before budgets blow out, and restrict high-cost frontier models to teams with a clear business case.
