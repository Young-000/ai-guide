**Summary**: Anthropic ends weeks of access uncertainty by locking in a permanent two-tier structure for Fable 5 as of July 20.

### Key Facts
- **Max and Team Premium**: Fable 5 permanently included at 50% of the weekly usage limit — no extra charge
- **Pro and Team Standard**: Fable 5 removed from included quota; access via usage credits only ($10/M input tokens, $50/M output tokens)
- Pro and Team Standard subscribers receive a one-time $100 credit to soften the transition
- Fable 5 had been provisionally included for all subscribers since its July 1 return after a US export-control period
- Daily $2,000 spend caps and auto-recharge settings must be configured separately

### Why It Matters
Today's change makes Fable 5 a Max-tier differentiator. At $50/M output tokens, a single active session can blow through a Pro subscription's monthly cost in hours, making the Max upgrade-versus-credits calculation urgent for heavy users. Strategically, the tiered access model lets Anthropic meter Fable 5 demand by plan level — a capacity-management lever that could reshape how users choose between Anthropic's subscription tiers.
