**Summary**: Anthropic's Claude Sonnet 5, released June 30, closes the gap between mid-tier and frontier models for agentic workloads — at introductory pricing of $2/$10 per million tokens.

### Key Facts
- Now the default model for Free and Pro tiers; immediately available on Max, Team, and Enterprise
- Introductory pricing: $2/M input, $10/M output (through August 31); standard pricing $3/$15 thereafter
- Autonomously plans and executes multi-step tasks using browsers, terminals, and external tools
- Substantial improvement over Sonnet 4.6 across reasoning, tool use, coding, and knowledge tasks

### Why It Matters
Until now, complex agentic workflows demanded Opus-tier models, adding significant cost and latency. Sonnet 5 brings near-frontier capability to a price point accessible for large-scale enterprise automation — a development that could reshape how organizations design and budget AI agent deployments.
