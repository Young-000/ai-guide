**Summary**: The Future of Life Institute evaluated nine major AI labs across six domains and 37 indicators — and awarded no A or B grades.

### Key Facts
- Anthropic: C+ (1st); OpenAI and Google DeepMind: C (tied 2nd); Meta: D+
- Z.ai and Alibaba Cloud: D-; xAI, DeepSeek, and Mistral: F
- Graded by a seven-member panel of independent AI researchers using absolute performance standards, not curves
- Core finding: Anthropic, OpenAI, Google DeepMind, and Meta have all weakened or abandoned earlier pledges to halt development if systems approach specified danger thresholds

### Why It Matters
The index is one of the first public, third-party audits of whether AI developers are keeping their own safety promises. The findings arrive weeks before the EU AI Act's high-risk obligations take effect on August 2, raising questions about whether voluntary commitments can serve as a credible governance baseline — or whether binding regulation is the only instrument with teeth.
