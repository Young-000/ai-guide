**Summary**: ChatGPT surpassed 1 billion monthly active users in May 2026, roughly 3.5 years after its mobile launch — a pace that leaves TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube well behind.

### Key facts
- **May 2026**: 1 billion MAU milestone confirmed via Sensor Tower data, reported by Reuters on June 2
- **Speed**: Faster than TikTok (~4 years), Instagram (~6 years), and YouTube (~8 years) to reach the same threshold
- **February 2026**: OpenAI had reported ~900 million weekly active users; jumped to 1 billion MAU just three months later
- **Counterpoint**: A Pew Research survey (March 2026) found over half of U.S. adults are more concerned than excited about AI — usage and sentiment are diverging

### Why it matters
One billion MAU means ChatGPT is no longer a curiosity — it's infrastructure. That scale strengthens OpenAI's negotiating position in enterprise contracts, advertising, and API pricing discussions. It also raises the competitive stakes for Anthropic, Google, and Meta, all of whom are racing to close the B2C gap before usage habits calcify around OpenAI's products.
