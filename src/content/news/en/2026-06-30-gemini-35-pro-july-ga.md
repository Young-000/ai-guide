**Summary**: Gemini 3.5 Pro missed its June window but is confirmed for July GA, bringing a 2-million-token context window — the largest in any production frontier model — and a new Deep Think reasoning mode.

### Key Facts
- **2M token context**: Double Gemini 3.5 Flash's ceiling, the largest deployed context among production frontier models as of June 2026
- **Deep Think reasoning**: Gated to the $250/month Ultra tier, designed to handle complex multi-step problems where current models often fail
- Currently in limited preview for select Vertex AI enterprise customers; Google is targeting **general availability in July**
- Competitive window: GPT-5.6 Sol/Terra/Luna remains locked to ~20 government-vetted partners; Claude Fable 5 is suspended — making Gemini 3.5 Pro the only accessible top-tier frontier model for most developers

### Why It Matters
With GPT-5.6 gated by the US government and Fable 5 still suspended, Gemini 3.5 Pro is poised to be the default frontier model for the majority of developers when it launches. The 2M token window has practical implications: full-codebase analysis, book-length document review, and long-horizon agent tasks that exceed every other model's ceiling.
