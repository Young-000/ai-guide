**Summary**: Anthropic launched Claude Sonnet 5 on June 30, 2026 — the most capable mid-tier model in its lineup, delivering reasoning, tool use, and coding performance that approaches Opus 4.8 at significantly lower cost.

### Key Facts
- API model ID: `claude-sonnet-5`; default model on Free and Pro plans
- Introductory pricing: $2 input / $10 output per million tokens through August 31, 2026; rising to $3/$15 thereafter
- Supports 1M token context window, up to 128k output tokens, and Adaptive Thinking by default
- Available across all plans (Free, Pro, Max, Team, Enterprise)

### Why It Matters
Claude Sonnet 5 compresses the performance gap between mid-tier and frontier models, giving developers a strong reason to replace Opus 4.8 in agentic pipelines where maximum throughput and cost efficiency matter. If the pricing trajectory holds, it signals another step in the ongoing commoditization of top-tier LLM performance — what needed a flagship model six months ago now fits a mid-range budget.
