**Summary**: Moonshot AI's Kimi K3 — 2.8 trillion parameters, the largest open-weight model ever released — jumped 17 places to top the Frontend Code Arena, surpassing Claude Fable 5.

### Key facts
- 2.8T parameters, the largest open-weight model to date; built despite US chip export restrictions targeting China
- Frontend Code Arena: #1 (1,679 pts), up from Kimi K2.6's 18th place; leads 6 of 7 front-end domains
- #1 in SWE Marathon and Program Bench; #2 in Terminal Bench 2.1 (half a point behind GPT-5.6 Sol)
- API pricing: $0.30/M cache-hit, $3/M cache-miss input, $15/M output; open weights release planned July 27

### Why it matters
Kimi K3 is the clearest signal yet that Chinese open-weight models have moved from competitive to best-in-class at coding — a domain central to enterprise AI adoption. The July 27 open-weights release will let any developer download and fine-tune the world's largest publicly available model, putting further pressure on closed proprietary providers.
