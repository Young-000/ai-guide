**Summary**: Anthropic's annualized revenue run rate surpassed $30 billion in Q1 2026, overtaking OpenAI on that metric and setting a new benchmark for AI startup growth.

### Key Facts
- ARR jumped from $87M in January 2024 to $30B by April 2026
- 80x single-quarter growth — Amodei: "We planned for 10x a year; we got 80x"
- Claude Code hit $1B annualized revenue within six months of public launch, the fastest-growing product in company history
- Compute demand forced Anthropic to lease capacity from Elon Musk's xAI data centers

### Why It Matters
These numbers confirm that enterprise AI has moved from pilot projects to core infrastructure. Anthropic now edges out OpenAI ($24B ARR as of March) on annualized run rate, reshaping the competitive narrative at the frontier of AI development.
