**Summary**: Chinese open-weight models have quietly captured the majority of open-source AI usage, even as the frontier race remains dominated by US labs.

### Key Facts
- Chinese open-weight models accounted for **41%** of all Hugging Face downloads over the trailing year — surpassing US models for the first time
- **61%** of tokens processed on OpenRouter came from Chinese models; the top 6 most popular models are all Chinese firms: Tencent, Xiaomi, DeepSeek, MiniMax, and Z.ai
- Alibaba's **Qwen** crossed **1 billion cumulative downloads** on Hugging Face, overtaking Meta's Llama as the all-time leader
- Tencent HY3 topped OpenRouter's weekly chart at 6.13 trillion tokens; it runs free in Codex CLI via a base-URL swap

### Why It Matters
The frontier race is still US-led, but the open-source layer — where most startups and developers actually build — has shifted decisively toward China. Price competitiveness, permissive licensing, and competitive performance are converging in Chinese open models, making them the de facto infrastructure layer of practical AI adoption worldwide.
