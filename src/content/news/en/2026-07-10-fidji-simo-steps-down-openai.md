**In one line**: Fidji Simo, effectively OpenAI's No. 2, is stepping down from her full-time role after a medical leave ran longer than expected.

### Key points
- Simo was OpenAI's top applications executive, overseeing the company's product and business side.
- The departure reportedly follows a medical leave that lasted longer than anticipated.
- The vacancy lands at a sensitive moment, as OpenAI eyes a possible IPO and races to catch Anthropic in the enterprise market.

### Why it matters
A gap in the No. 2 role could strain OpenAI's business operations and IPO preparations. With enterprise competition intensifying, how quickly the company names a successor and stabilizes the organization may shape its execution in the months ahead.
