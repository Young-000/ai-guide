**One-line summary**: Anthropic's annualized revenue crossed $30 billion as of April 2026 — 80x growth in a single quarter — with Claude Code emerging as the company's fastest-growing product ever.

### Highlights
- **$30B ARR milestone**: Run rate surged from $9B (end-2025) to $30B by April 2026. CEO Dario Amodei described it as "crazy" growth.
- **Claude Code as growth engine**: The agentic coding tool launched in mid-2025 now contributes over $2.5B ARR on its own, becoming the company's fastest-growing product.
- **Enterprise flywheel**: Customers spending over $1M/year doubled to more than 1,000 in under two months; enterprise accounts for the majority of revenue.
- **Compute build-out**: Expanded partnership with Google Cloud and Broadcom to secure gigawatts of TPU capacity starting 2027.

### Why it matters
Anthropic's rapid rise — reportedly surpassing OpenAI in revenue for the first time — signals a structural shift in enterprise AI procurement. Claude Code's breakout growth confirms that agentic coding tools have become the top investment priority for software organizations, reshaping the competitive dynamics of the entire AI landscape.
