**Summary**: Anthropic is funding a structured pipeline that puts AI-literate early-career workers inside nonprofits at zero cost to host organizations, co-run with CodePath and Social Finance.

### Key Facts
- **Scale**: 1,000 fellows over three cohorts; $150M total investment; Anthropic, CodePath, and Social Finance co-manage
- **Compensation**: $85,000/year salary plus full benefits — entirely covered by Anthropic; host organizations pay nothing
- **Fellow support**: CodePath mentor, Anthropic technical office hours, expanded Claude token budget, and a manager at the host org
- **Timeline**: Cohort 1 (~100 fellows) begins October 2026; Cohorts 2 and 3 follow January and August 2027; applications close July 17

### Why It Matters
AI capability has largely pooled inside well-funded tech companies and large enterprises. Claude Corps is a direct attempt to route that capacity into mission-driven organizations — education, environment, civic services — that lack the hiring budgets to recruit AI talent on their own. If it scales as planned, it also gives Anthropic a broad, real-world deployment feedback loop outside enterprise SaaS.
