**Summary**: Day 18 of the Fable 5 suspension — sources say multiple US agencies have signed off on restoring general access, with only the Pentagon and NSA outstanding.

### Key Facts
- Multiple agencies within the Trump administration have backed a full Fable 5 restoration; **Pentagon and NSA signatures are the sole remaining block**
- Mythos 5 was partially restored June 26 for ~100 vetted US critical-infrastructure partners including Apple, Google, Cisco, Nvidia, and Microsoft (Project Glasswing members)
- Anthropic is estimated to be losing roughly **$9M per day** in revenue during the suspension; developers have shifted en masse to Claude Sonnet and Haiku
- A restored Fable 5 would still operate under the June 12 export-control framework — civil and criminal penalty provisions remain in force

### Why It Matters
Fable 5 held the top position for coding and agentic workflows before its suspension. The speed of return — and whether geographic or use-case restrictions accompany it — will directly affect Anthropic's IPO timeline and set a precedent for how governments can mandate AI access controls globally.
