**Summary**: Reflection AI has locked in a second massive compute deal in two months, this time with European AI cloud Nebius, to accelerate training of open frontier models at scale.

### Key Facts
- Reflection AI (valued at **$8 billion**) signed a **$1B+** computing agreement with Nebius, running through **end of 2029**
- Deal provides access to **Nvidia's latest chips** for large-scale frontier model training
- Second major compute deal in two months: June's SpaceX Colossus 2 contract is worth up to $6.3B ($150M/month through 2029)
- Co-founded by ex-Google DeepMind researchers; aims to build Western open-source alternatives to proprietary frontier models

### Why It Matters
Frontier model training has historically been the exclusive domain of hyperscalers. Reflection's rapid accumulation of independent compute capacity — from two separate providers in two months — signals that well-capitalized open-source labs are mounting a credible challenge. The open frontier race is no longer hypothetical; it is being resourced. Nebius stock (NBIS) climbed 3% on the announcement.
