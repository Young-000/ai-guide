**Summary**: The one-week window that let Claude Pro, Max, and Team subscribers use Fable 5 for up to half their weekly allowance ends today. Starting July 8, the flagship model is access-by-credit-only — no loaded balance means no access.

### Key facts
- July 1–7: Fable 5 bundled into Pro/Max/Team/select Enterprise at up to 50% of weekly usage limits
- From July 8: exclusive access via prepaid usage credits at $10/M input and $50/M output tokens
- Requests will fail if no credit balance is available
- Controls available: $2,000 daily redemption cap, configurable monthly spending limit, optional auto-reload
- Anthropic says it intends to restore Fable 5 to subscriptions "as soon as capacity allows" — no timeline given

### Why it matters
The grace period obscured Fable 5's real cost. At $50/M output tokens, even moderate conversational use can exceed a full Pro subscription in a matter of days. Users who rely on Fable 5 for agentic or long-context work should load credits and configure spending caps today to avoid sudden access loss.
