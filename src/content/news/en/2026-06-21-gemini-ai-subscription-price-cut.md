**Summary**: Google repriced its entire Gemini subscription lineup, cutting the entry tier to $4.99/month — one cent below ChatGPT Go — and bundling twice the cloud storage to sweeten the deal.

### Key Facts
- **AI Plus**: $7.99 → $4.99/month; cloud storage doubled from 200 GB to 400 GB
- **AI Ultra**: $250 → $200/month; 20× higher usage limit retained
- **New $100/month Ultra tier**: 5× usage, 20 TB storage, YouTube Premium, priority Antigravity access
- Gemini 3.5 Flash is now the default model in the Gemini app and AI Mode in Search

### Why It Matters
The price war in consumer AI subscriptions is now explicit. Google is betting that ecosystem bundling — storage, YouTube, and search integration — can convert users who've hesitated to pay for AI. With ChatGPT's market share already below 50% for the first time, a meaningful price gap in Google's favor could accelerate the shift.
