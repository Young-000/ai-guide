**Summary**: Two of the world's largest IT services firms chose Anthropic Claude as their AI standard within a single day, a signal that enterprise AI consolidation is accelerating.

### Key Facts
- **DXC Technology** (June 11): joined Anthropic's Claude Partner Network as a Global Premier partner; already uses Claude internally via its OASIS platform where **95% of code is Claude-generated**, cutting software delivery time roughly 10x
- **TCS** (June 12): will train **50,000 employees** across engineering, finance, legal, and sales on Claude; launching a dedicated business unit to build industry-specific AI solutions on Claude
- Both firms collectively serve thousands of regulated-industry clients — adoption as a default tool means cascading volume for Anthropic
- DXC's initial verticals: insurance, cybersecurity, application services

### Why It Matters
The IT services layer is where AI adoption scales from proof-of-concept to infrastructure. DXC and TCS don't just use software — they run the core systems of banks, airlines, and governments. Their commitment to Claude as a production standard means Claude is moving from developer tool to enterprise backbone, which will sharply accelerate Anthropic's revenue growth and entrench Claude in industries that move slowly and switch rarely.
