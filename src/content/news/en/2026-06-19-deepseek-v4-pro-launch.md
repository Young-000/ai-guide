**Summary**: DeepSeek launched V4 Pro on April 24, 2026 — repeating the V3 playbook at trillion-parameter scale by pairing near-frontier coding performance with open weights and aggressive pricing.

### Key Facts
- **Architecture**: 1.6T total / 49B active parameters (Mixture-of-Experts); 1M-token context window; up to 384K output tokens; thinking and non-thinking modes
- **Pricing**: $0.435/M input, $0.87/M output — approximately one-tenth of GPT-5.5's output price
- **Benchmarks**: 80.6% on SWE-bench Verified, placing it among the top coding models available
- **License**: MIT — fully open for commercial use and self-hosting; still in preview as of June 2026

### Why It Matters
DeepSeek V4 Pro proves the V3 thesis at a larger scale: open weights, frontier-class coding capability, and commodity pricing — available for on-premises deployment. That combination puts direct cost pressure on OpenAI and Anthropic's enterprise offerings and lowers the barrier for teams that want powerful coding AI without vendor lock-in or per-token API costs.
