**Summary**: Google has ended a six-year smart speaker hiatus with a Gemini-native Home Speaker, priced at $99 and shipping June 25 — positioning it as a conversational AI hub rather than a voice remote.

### Key Facts
- Priced at $99, available in four colors (Hazel, Porcelain, Jade, Berry), general availability June 25
- Hardware: 58mm omnidirectional full-range driver, three far-field microphones, 360-degree sound, LED underglow ring
- "Gemini for Home" enables multi-step natural language conversations and chained smart home commands across devices
- 3.5 million early-access users generated feedback that drove 2,500+ bug fixes before launch

### Why It Matters
Google spent years rolling Gemini AI updates to older Nest devices before committing to new hardware — a disciplined approach that means this speaker arrives polished rather than rushed. At $99 it undercuts Apple's HomePod while positioning Gemini as the premier home AI interface, directly escalating the AI speaker battle with Amazon Alexa and Apple Siri.
