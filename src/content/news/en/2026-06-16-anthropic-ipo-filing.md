**Summary**: Anthropic filed a confidential Form S-1 with the SEC, kicking off what could be the largest AI IPO in history — just days after closing a $65B funding round and more than a week before rival OpenAI did the same.

### Key Facts
- **June 1, 2026**: Confidential draft Form S-1 submitted to the SEC
- Implied valuation: approximately **$965 billion** — near the $1 trillion mark
- **$65 billion** Series H funding round closed immediately before the filing
- Share count and offering price are not yet set; timing depends on market conditions
- Filed **seven days ahead** of OpenAI's own confidential submission on June 8

### Why It Matters
Two of the world's most valuable AI companies entering the public markets simultaneously signals that frontier AI has graduated from venture-backed bets to mainstream Wall Street assets. Anthropic's near-trillion valuation will set a benchmark that shapes how all frontier AI investment is priced going forward.
