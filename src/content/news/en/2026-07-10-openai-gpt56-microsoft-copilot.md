**In one line**: OpenAI said its new GPT-5.6 family will keep serving as the "preferred model" behind Microsoft's Copilot 365 workplace and productivity apps.

### Key points
- OpenAI confirmed the GPT-5.6 family will continue to power Microsoft's suite of workplace and productivity apps.
- The "preferred model" framing signals GPT-5.6's default status inside Copilot 365.
- The statement lands amid recent "breakup chatter" about the two companies' partnership.

### Why it matters
Microsoft has reportedly been weighing its own and third-party models alongside OpenAI's. A public confirmation of GPT-5.6's default role reads as an effort to defend OpenAI's largest distribution channel.
